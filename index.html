About Us

Warm Ambiance. Quality Dishes. Heartfelt Service.

Del Rio's Cafe is a welcoming local restaurant and cafe in Malaybalay City, crafted for people who enjoy comforting food and a relaxed atmosphere. We serve fresh meals, handcrafted coffee, desserts, smoothies, pasta, and all-day favorites.

Whether you are starting your morning, meeting friends for lunch, enjoying a date night, or taking a quiet coffee break, our team is here to make every visit memorable.
